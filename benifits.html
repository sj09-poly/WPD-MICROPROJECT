Benefits of Seminar on AI and Jobs

1. Understanding AI: Helps students learn what Artificial Intelligence is and how it works in real life.

2. Career Awareness: Provides information about job opportunities in AI such as data scientist, AI engineer, and machine learning expert.

3. Skill Development: Encourages learning new skills like programming, data analysis, and problem-solving.

4. Future Job Trends: Explains how AI is changing industries and what jobs will be in demand in the future.

5. Practical Knowledge: Gives real-world examples of how AI is used in companies like automation, chatbots, and robotics.

6. Innovation & Creativity: Motivates students to think creatively and build new AI-based solutions.

7. Networking Opportunities: Helps connect with experts, teachers, and other students interested in AI.

8. Confidence Building: Improves confidence by gaining knowledge and participating in discussions.
